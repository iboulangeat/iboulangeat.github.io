My research focusses on understanding the dynamics of socio-ecosystems, from a theoretical viewpoint to conservation issues in alpine ecosystems. I seak to improve biodiversity modelling over time and space without neglecting the interactions with the society. I aim to identify the properties supporting the resilience of those complex socio-ecosystems.
