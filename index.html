Research interests

My research focusses on understanding species distributions and community dynamics in terrestrial ecosystems, from a theoretical viewpoint to conservation issues. I build models or analyse datasets to explore how abiotic factors, dispersal, biotic interactions and disturbances interact and affect various biodiversity facets of the landscape, with a particular interest in applications to artic or alpine ecosystems.
